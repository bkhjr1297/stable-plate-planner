# AdSense Setup Checklist

Status: **ready, not active**

The site includes AdSense-safe placeholders, but ads are intentionally inactive until Brian completes Google AdSense account/site approval and provides the public Publisher ID.

## Brian-only sensitive steps

Brian must personally handle:

- Google account login
- AdSense account creation
- Identity verification
- Tax forms
- Banking/payout setup
- Policy/legal agreements
- Final site submission/approval

## Activation steps after approval

1. Copy the AdSense Publisher ID, usually shaped like `ca-pub-1234567890123456`.
2. Create at least one display ad unit in AdSense and copy its `data-ad-slot` ID.
3. Send Annabelle only the Publisher ID and ad-slot ID — not password, tax, banking, or identity details.
4. Annabelle will replace the inactive placeholders in `index.html` and selected blog templates.
5. Submit/verify the public site in AdSense if Google requires it.
6. Keep ad density low: one placement near the lower page body is enough during early validation.

## Policy guardrails

- No fake clicks.
- No encouraging users to click ads.
- No ads above or inside critical safety disclaimers.
- No misleading ad labels.
- Health-adjacent pages must keep claims conservative.
- If a site is not approved, leave placeholders inactive and keep building traffic/content.
