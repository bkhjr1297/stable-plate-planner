Budget Grocery List for More Stable Blood Sugar

Published 2026-05-26 • Target keyword: budget stable blood sugar grocery list

A cheap low-glycemic grocery framework built around protein, fiber, and slow carbs.

Build from low-cost anchors

Eggs, canned tuna, chicken thighs, Greek yogurt, tofu, beans, lentils, and cottage cheese can cover most protein needs without specialty products.

Add fiber volume

Frozen broccoli, cabbage, spinach, cucumbers, peppers, carrots, berries, and salad greens help meals feel larger while slowing digestion.

Keep carbs deliberate

Use beans, lentils, oats, quinoa, sweet potato, berries, or small portions of brown rice instead of defaulting to bread, chips, juice, or sweets.
