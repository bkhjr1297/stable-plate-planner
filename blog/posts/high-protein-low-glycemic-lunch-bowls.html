High-Protein Low-Glycemic Lunch Bowls

Published 2026-05-26 • Target keyword: high protein low glycemic lunch bowls

A repeatable lunch bowl formula for workdays, meal prep, and steady afternoon energy.

Use a bowl formula

Choose one protein, two vegetables, one fat, and one slow carb. This creates variety without needing a new recipe every day.

Three quick combinations

Chicken with greens, cucumber, avocado, and beans; tuna with romaine, pickles, olive oil, and lentil soup; tofu with frozen vegetables and cauliflower rice.

Pack without overthinking

Keep dressing separate, make two proteins at once, and use frozen vegetables when fresh produce would go bad before you can use it.
