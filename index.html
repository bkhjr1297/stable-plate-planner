The 3-rule stable plate method

This tool keeps the method intentionally simple so it can be used when energy is low.

1. Anchor protein

Start with eggs, chicken, turkey, fish, Greek yogurt, tofu, beans, or lean beef. Protein reduces random snacking and supports training.

2. Add fiber volume

Use greens, broccoli, peppers, cabbage, berries, avocado, chia, or lentils to slow digestion and increase fullness.

3. Choose slow carbs carefully

When carbs are needed, favor oats, beans, lentils, quinoa, sweet potato, berries, or small portions of brown rice.
