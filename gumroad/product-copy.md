## Short description
A personalized low-glycemic, high-protein meal rotation built around your foods, budget, cooking access, and schedule.

## Full description
The free Stable Plate Planner gives quick meal ideas. This custom plan turns that structure into a practical meal rotation built around your real life.

You will receive:

- A personalized stable plate meal rotation
- Simple grocery list
- High-protein, fiber-forward structure
- Low-prep options when energy is low
- Budget-conscious substitutions where possible
- Clear notes on foods to avoid based on your preferences

Best for people who want:

- Steadier energy
- Cleaner meal structure
- Low-glycemic meal ideas
- Simple grocery planning
- Less decision fatigue

Important: This is educational meal-planning support only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, insulin dosing advice, or a replacement for your clinician's care plan. If you have diabetes or another medical condition, follow your medical team's instructions.

## Buyer instructions / custom fields
Ask the buyer for:

1. Email/contact method
2. Which plan they want: 3-day, 7-day, or monthly refresh
3. Main goal: stable energy, weight loss support, muscle/high-protein, budget, low-prep
4. Foods they like
5. Foods they avoid / allergies
6. Cooking access: no cooking, microwave, stove/oven, air fryer, full kitchen
7. Approximate grocery budget
8. Number of people eating from the plan
9. Any general notes — no private medical records or medication dosing details
